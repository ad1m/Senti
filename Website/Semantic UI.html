About Us

Sentimint is a tool to easily capture real-time twitter sentiment for any keyword. Gather sentiment on publicly traded financial companies, recent discoveries, the latest tech gadgets, sports figures such as LeBron James, musicians like Justin Bieber, current political issues like immigration, and much more. Discover the trending topics of the day and how the public percieves their sentiment. Every time you search a keyword, our algorithm runs against Twitter feed data and captures the outlook of the tweets. In seconds, you will have the current public opinion for any given keyword. See how sentiment has evolved over time with historical sentiment charts.

How We Do It

Everytime you search a keyword, our algorithm scans the most recent tweets and uses our advanced sentiment analysis tool kit to parse through the tweets to find how positive or negative the tweet is, based on a numerical scale. The data is then presented to you without the hassle of developing time consuming code.
